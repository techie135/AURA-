What is AURA?

AURA is an AI-powered emotion recognition and smart-environment automation system designed to create human-centric, emotionally adaptive spaces.

It uses Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to understand facial expressions and voice tone, and integrates with IoT devices to automatically adjust your environment based on your emotional state.
